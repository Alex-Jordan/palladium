Checkpoint 1.3.11.

(a)

Use the data in Example 10 to find the rate of change in Oregon cancer diagnoses between 2007 and 2017.
🔗
Explanation.
To find the rate of change between 2007 and 2017, calculate
🔗
\begin{equation*} \frac{23052 - 19417}{2017 - 2007} = 363.5 \text{.} \end{equation*}
🔗
So the rate of change was \({363.5\ {\textstyle\frac{\rm\mathstrut diagnoses}{\rm\mathstrut yr}}}\text{.}\)
