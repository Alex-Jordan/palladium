Process 3.5.17. Solving Rational Equations.

To solve a rational equation,
  1. Find the least common denominator for all terms in the equation.
    🔗
    🔗
  2. Multiply every term in the equation by the least common denominator
    🔗
    🔗
  3. Every denominator should cancel leaving a simpler kind of equation to solve. Use previous method to solve that equation.
