A rate is a measurement that tells us how one quantity changes with respect to how some other quantity is changing. For example, the number of people on earth is growing by about \(2.6\) people per second. Time is passing second by second, and as each second passes, we gain of about \(2.6\) people. The rate here is \(2.6\,\frac{\text{people}}{\text{s}}\text{.}\)
