Commutative Property.

To find the area of a rectangle, multiply the length by the width. Does the result change if we multiply the width by the length?

There is a similar property for addition, which you can see in the equation \(1+2=2+1\text{.}\) Itâ€™s called the commutative property of addition. However, subtraction and division do not have the commutative property, because for example \(2-1\ne1-2\) and \(\frac{4}{2}\ne\frac{2}{4}\text{.}\)
